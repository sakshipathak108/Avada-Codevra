1.What is the difference between the ‘find a team’ and ‘join a team’ sections?

The ‘find a team’ section is for those who want to form a new team for a particular event. They will have to fill up the form given in that section in order to get their profiles displayed to potential teammates in the ‘Join a team’ section. The ‘Join a team’ section is for those who are simply looking to join a pre existing team. A user can search for a particular event or profile in the search engine and all the relevant profile cards will be visible, out of which the user can now send a request on the profile they are interested in.


2.Is it necessary to login if we are anyways filling up the ‘Find a teammate’ form?

Yes, you have to login on the site in order to create your profile and get your dashboard. ‘Find a Team mate’ form is optional,only if u r looking to create a team.


3.How do I send a request to join a team?

In the ‘Join a team’ section,click on the ‘Ask to Join’ option in order to let the person know that you are interested in joining their team.

4.How does the person I have asked to join get to see my profile?

When you click on the ‘Ask to Join’ option on the profile card,your profile is directly sent to the ‘People Interested’ section of the ‘Requests’ section of the receiver’s dashboard.


5.How do I keep track of the number of requests I have sent?

All the profiles that you have asked to join will be visible in the ‘Your Sent Requests’ section of the ‘Requests’ section of the receiver’s dashboard.


6.What if we want to participate in an event not listed in the drop down list?

Though this situation might not occur as our team keeps tabs on the ongoing events of our college,however, you may contact us through any of the given links,we will add the events accordingly.


7.In case I don’t want to participate in a given event anymore, how do I delete my form?

Under the events section of your dashboard,all the events for which you have filled up the form are listed along with a delete option. When you click on the delete option,your form along with your profile card for the given event is deleted.


8.How can we reach you in case of a technical problem?

You may contact us through any of the given social media links, we shall be more than willing to help!
